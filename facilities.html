System Requirements

Minimum Requirements

  • Computer/Laptop or Smartphone
  • Internet connection (2 Mbps minimum)
  • Webcam and Microphone (for live classes)
  • Updated web browser (Chrome/Firefox/Safari)

Recommended Setup

  • Dedicated study space at home
  • Broadband internet (5 Mbps or higher)
  • Headphones for better audio quality
  • Tablet or second screen for note-taking
